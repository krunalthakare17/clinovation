Patient Registration
    The patient's name, address, contact information, date of birth, employment, and insurance information are the first pieces of crucial data that the patient registration module of the dental software eDentra is intended to collect for the patient information chart.
    The module automatically confirms patients' benefits eligibility through secured electronic data interchange, assuring proper billing and lowering the possibility of errors. It assigns a distinct patient ID for recording visits, enabling simple record keeping and retrieval.
    The system notifies patients of impending appointments via SMS text messages or emails, reducing the possibility of no-shows.
